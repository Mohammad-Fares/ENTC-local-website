About


Baking Wholesome Treats Since 1980

Since 1980, Specialty Donuts has been freshly baking wholesome treats for our cherished patrons. Our mission is to deliver moments of joy and indulgence with every bite - we’re committed to care and tradition; crafting sweet and savoury goods for youthful sweet-tooths and those after a simple, yet delicious savoury bite.

Donuts are at the forefront of our offering, with classic glazed donuts, eclairs and more filling the displays. But the sweetness doesn’t end there - fluffy muffins are also baked fresh daily; best paired with freshly brewed coffee or tea.

To complement these delightful treats, Specialty Donuts takes pride in serving toasted sandwiches, wraps, and open-melts, to satisfy all palates.
