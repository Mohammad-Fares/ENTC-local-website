GENERAL FAQ


Do you only sell donuts?


We have a wide range of donuts, as well as muffins, sandwiches and wraps. We also serve beverages to sip on alongside your treats!


Are your donuts made in-house?


Yes they are! All our donuts and muffins are baked on-site daily.


Are any of your products allergen-free?


We do not recommend our products to anyone with severe allergies.


Do you offer gift-cards?


Yes we offer gift cards in-store only. Please head to one of our locations for more.


Is there seating available at your stores?


Our stores are mainly set up for takeaway, however we do have very limited seating available at our Macarthur Square and Oran Park locations.


PRE-ORDER FAQ


Do you deliver?


Unfortunately, we do not offer delivery at this time. All orders will need to be picked up from your chosen store.


How far in advance do we need to enquire about pre-order items?


Please provide at least 24-48 hours in advance to fulfil any pre-order enquiries. Please fill out our pre-order form here or contact your local store for last-minute orders.


Which stores offer pickup?


All our stores offer pick-ups for any pre-order items!


What time do we pick up our order?


Orders can be picked up anytime from 9am until closing. Please inform us upon ordering on your chosen pick up time so we can ensure they're as fresh as possible


Do you honour special requests?


We do offer custom colours and decor, as well as cake toppers for an additional charge. Please enquire for more information.
